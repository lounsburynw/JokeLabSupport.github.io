Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use Joke Lab?

Joke Lab is easy to use! Just open the app, select a voice, and hit the "Tell me a joke" button.

Can I share the jokes?

Yes, you can! Use the share buttons to share audio clips and joke transcripts with your friends.

Is Joke Lab free?

Joke Lab is free for a trial period. Afterwards, it costs a monthly subscription.

Help, my app isn't responding

Ensure you are logged into your iCloud account.
